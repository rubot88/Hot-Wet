О бренде Hot&Wet

Style Heroine’s Founder and Creative Director, Evangelie Smyrniotaki, has us lusting for our next tropical vacation. She recently traveled to sunny Maldives where the crystal clear water and white sandy beaches served as a perfect backdrop to our new soft pastel resort wear pieces. Evangelie has an impeccable eye for styling and image directing. “I believe the greatest trick is the environment and the swimwear. If you have stunning swimwear and a great environment, it’s quite difficult not to have great images.” A favorite style of Evangelie’s is the Palm Springs Bikini because it “reminds me of the absolute chicness of the 50’s.” She also has warm weather packing down. “Try to pack more dresses and jumpsuits than separate pieces because they are going to save you lots of space. A cotton dress that will take me from day to night, a pair of slides and of course a book, a great love story.
